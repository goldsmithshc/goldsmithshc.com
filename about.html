About Us

Team of the Year 2017/18

THE CLUB

The Goldsmiths, University of London Hockey Club was founded in 2014 after the merge of the Men’s and Women’s clubs. We are a club of one mixed team competing in the London Universities Sports League (LUSL) Mixed Hockey leagues on Sundays. We also play against our local clubs and universities on Wednesday.

We train at the home of English hockey at the Lee Valley Hockey & Tennis Centre in the Queen Elizabeth Olympic Park, who most recently played host to the Hockey Women’s World Cup 2018. Every March, arguably our biggest event on our calendar is our varsity against University of Arts, London as part of “The Arts Cup”. Last season saw a tight defeat, but we are looking to set the record straight this year.

In 2017/18, we came joint-second in Division 1, and awarded the Sports Team of the Year by our Students Union for our work in “creating a fun, competitive team where both new and experienced players have a chance to show their skills and excel”.

In 2018/19, we have been drafted in the LUSL Mixed Hockey Premier Division, competing against the likes of UCL, LSE, and Queen Mary. We’re also planning to continue our plethora of social events including ice skating, social nights and a tour. Get in touch with any of the committee if you have any questions.
